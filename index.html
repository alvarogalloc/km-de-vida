Kilómetros de Vida

Ningún plato desperdiciado. Ningúna persona olvidada.
In GDL there is up to a 25% waste across the whole food production chain

That means that a quarter of all the food that we produce never even reaches people’s plates

Households are the main source of food waste in Mexico

This is caused by inadequate meal planning, lack of awareness about date labels, improper storage, etc.

Enough food is produced in Guadalajara to feed its entire population

The problem lies in the lack of distribution
